Escape Room - Unity VR

Project Overview

The Escape Room app is an immersive virtual reality experience developed using Unity, where users can explore and solve puzzles within various themed rooms. The app allows players to interact with objects, uncover hidden clues, and complete challenges in a fully interactive environment. With an intuitive design, it offers a captivating and engaging experience, ideal for players seeking a thrilling escape room adventure in VR.

Project Details

Duration: April 22th 2024 - August 2nd 2024

Key Achievements

  • Developed an immersive Escape Room app in Unity VR, featuring interactive puzzles and themed rooms
  • Created Rooms for players to explore and solve challenges, providing a dynamic and engaging experience
  • Gained hands-on experience in Unity and VR development for creating interactive and immersive environments
  • Enhanced skills in managing 3D objects, interactions, and immersive gameplay within a virtual reality setting
