Gorlock's Paradise Figma

Project Overview

Gorlock's Paradise is a food reservation app designed to provide users with a seamless experience for booking tables at their favorite restaurants, exploring menu options, and managing their dining preferences with ease.

Project Details

Duration: November 15th 2024 - November 29th 2024

Type: Figma Mobile App Design Mini Project

Key Achievements

  • Designed an intuitive and user-friendly interface to simplify the reservation process
  • Created interactive prototypes to visualize user flows and improve functionality
  • Gained hands-on experience in UI/UX design, focusing on user engagement and accessibility
